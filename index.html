Tracking Trump's Conflicts of Interest

Details and news coverage of the Trump Family’s business and financial dealings

This database is part of the Sunlight Foundation’s ongoing “Tracking Trump’s Conflicts of Interest” project, funded by the Lodestar Foundation. As we continue to learn about the First Family’s business holdings, this database will be updated. Learn more about the project or our methodology and download the data. You can also contact us if you’re familiar with any of the conflicts we’re tracking.
